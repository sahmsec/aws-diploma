FAQ

Common Questions

What are the prerequisites?

HSC passed in any group (Science, Arts, or Commerce). Diploma students and Undergraduates from any background are welcome.

Is this course fully Virtual?

Yes, all classes are conducted Virtual via live sessions. 2 classes per week, 2 hours each, plus 3 hours weekly support.

How many certifications will I receive?

4 certifications: Cyber Security & Ethical Hacking, Cyber Security with Python, Advanced Cyber Security, and the Professional Diploma.

Certification Rule

Students must demonstrate real-world cybersecurity practice by earning at least USD 100 through verified sources such as remote work, freelancing, or bug bounty programs.

Who is this program for?

HSC/Diploma graduates, university students, job seekers, career switchers, IT professionals, developers, and entrepreneurs.
