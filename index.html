About Us

Government Recognized Technical Education Institute

Arena Web Security is a government-recognized technical education institute in Bangladesh, officially registered and accredited by the Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB) and the National Skills Development Authority (NSDA), Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh.

We operate as an authorized technical and vocational education provider, delivering structured academic and professional diploma programs in Cyber Security in alignment with national education policies and skills development frameworks.

Our academic activities are focused on curriculum-based instruction, laboratory-oriented learning, and applied cybersecurity education designed to meet both national and international standards.

Academic Background & Institutional Legacy

With over 14 years of academic journey, Arena Web Security has been actively contributing to the development of cybersecurity education in Bangladesh. Throughout this period, the institute has focused on building technically competent cybersecurity professionals through curriculum-driven instruction, laboratory-based training, and applied research practices.

Our graduates are equipped with strong foundational knowledge and practical exposure aligned with global cybersecurity standards, contributing to the strengthening of digital security awareness and capacity building within the country.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with practical cybersecurity skills through innovative education, hands-on training, and industry partnerships, creating professionals who can defend digital infrastructures against evolving threats.

We strive to make cybersecurity education accessible, practical, and aligned with global standards.

Our Vision

To become the leading cybersecurity education hub in the region, recognized for producing world-class security professionals who drive innovation and secure the digital future.

We envision a world where every organization has access to skilled cybersecurity talent.

FAQ

Common Questions

What are the prerequisites?

HSC passed in any group (Science, Arts, or Commerce). Diploma students and Undergraduates from any background are welcome.

How many certifications will I receive?

4 certifications: Cyber Security & Ethical Hacking, Cyber Security with Python, Advanced Cyber Security, and the Professional Diploma.

Certification Rule

Students must demonstrate real-world cybersecurity practice and complete an internship to be eligible for certification.

Who is this program for?

HSC/Diploma graduates, university students, job seekers, career switchers, IT professionals, developers, and entrepreneurs.
